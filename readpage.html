The Silent Patient

By Alex Michaelides

The Silent Patient by Alex Michaelides is a gripping psychological thriller that follows Alicia Berenson, a famous painter who seemingly has a perfect life—until she suddenly shoots her husband five times and never speaks another word. Confined to a secure psychiatric facility, Alicia's silence turns her into a mystery that intrigues psychotherapist Theo Faber. Determined to unravel the truth behind her crime, Theo delves into her past, uncovering dark secrets and shocking twists that challenge everything he believes. With its unpredictable narrative and psychological depth, The Silent Patient keeps readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.
